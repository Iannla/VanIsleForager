Why Forage?

  1. Save yourself some money! 1 kg of store apples $3.70. 1 kg foraged apples FREE.
  2. Holistic Health: pick an apple, calm your mind; eat an apple, nourish your body; connect with the tree that feeds you, enrich your spirit; plant the seed, spread the seed, let life grow.
  3. Better for the environment! Reduces the pollution that results from transporting food from other countries
  4. Taste the local flavours of your neighbourhood. It may not the brightest apple but it’s bursting with flavour.
  5. By providing food for yourself, you become more self-sufficient. A measure of self-sufficiency is needed for a resilient food system into today’s globalized context. What would you do if grocery stores stopped providing produce?
  6. It is fun and feels darn good. Get out and make friends within the community and become a steward for your neighbourhood.
